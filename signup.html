Account Requirements

Username:

  • At least 8 characters long
  • At least 1 uppercase letter and 1 lowercase letter
  • At least 1 number
  • At least 1 special character
  • No spaces

Password:

  • At least 8 characters long
  • At least 1 uppercase letter and 1 lowercase letter
  • At least 1 number
  • At least 1 special character
  • No spaces
  • Can not be the same as the username
